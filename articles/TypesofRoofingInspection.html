The SkyCam - Types of Roofing Inspection

 

There are many types of roofing inspection, we divide them into the following broad categories:

  • General Condition Inspection - understand and record the general condition of your roof
  • Problem Diagnosis - investigate a specific issue
  • Quality Assurance - check the quality and effectiveness of work

General Condition Inspection

This is the most common and most comprehensive type of inspection, giving a complete view of the roof and its main features. This is often used as part of a formal property survey.

Regular condition inspections give a view of how a roof is wearing and help in planning preventitive maintenance, to avoid longer term damage and the associated costs.

Problem Diagnosis

You may already know that there is a problem, but an aerial inspection can help you to accurately diagnose both the cause and location of the root problem.

This can save time and money by avoiding building scaffolding towers unnecessarily or in the wrong place.

Quality Assurance

When work has been done it can be important to check that the work meets expectations in terms of both quality and effectiveness.

In short this can tell you whether or not a problem has really gone away.
